Wired

In this section, a list of advantages of both wired and wireless medias in each area will be listed and a conclusion will be made for which is overall the most beneficial.

  • All bandwidth can be used on a network through an ethernet cable
  • As more devices are added, the network speed is unaffected
  • Faster consistent connections
  • Can connect network miles apart via copper or fibre optic
  • Wired networks are far more secure as they cannot be accessed remotely
  • Easy to identify connected devices
